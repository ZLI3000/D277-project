Welcome to the Golden State!!

  1. California is the most populous state in the U.S. and boasts one of the largest economies globally.
  2. From the tech hub of Silicon Valley to the entertainment capital of Hollywood, California is a leader in innovation, culture, and natural beauty.
  3. California offers a plethora of nature escapes with renowned national parks like Yosemite, breathtaking beaches along 800 miles of coastline, and picture perfect lakes such as Lake Tahoe.
General State Information
  • California has a population of 38,899,800 people. Making us the most populous state in the US.
  • We are home to the 49'ers, Los Angeles Lakers, and Golden State Warriors, making it a hub for major sporting events.
  • California represents world-renowned universities like Stanford, UC Berkeley, and UCLA.
  • Enjoy the cost of living here, the median income in California is $84,000.
